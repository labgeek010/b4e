Birdwatching Experience in Costa Rica

Discover Costa Rica's flying treasures.

Costa Rica stands as a birdwatching haven, boasting a remarkable array of over 900 bird species. Nestled between the Caribbean and Pacific coasts, this biodiverse paradise offers a unique blend of ecosystems, making it a prime destination for avid bird enthusiasts.

Diverse Bird Species in Costa Rica

Costa Rica is home to a spectacular variety of bird species, from vibrant toucans and resplendent quetzals to rare hummingbirds and majestic eagles. Each region, including Monteverde, Osa, Rincon de la Vieja, and Braulio Carrillo, unveils its own avian wonders, creating an unparalleled birdwatching experience.
